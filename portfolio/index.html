Animated Pixel Books The MAGIC of Books Brought to LIFE!

Video Tour

Pixel Books are meticulous crafted to simulated ultra realistic books. From the hard cover, the spine, the realistic multiple page flipping, and right down to the texture of the paper, we have pour in lots of effort in making books on computers a real experience.

When it comes to simulating books, it is getting pass the uncanny valley that creates that emotional connection between readers and books. And that's what we have done with the help of some technical wizardry. It is in these details where the magic happens!

View on the Big Screen

The best way to view Pixel Books is on the big TV screen, where the books are transformed into gigantic sizes that kids only see in movies and wished they own some themselves.

Besides big TV screens, Pixel Books can also be viewed on the computer, iPad, and any mobile devices. Any books our readers purchased can be view across all these platforms.

Dedicated Library

While you can hook your computer up to your big TV screen to read Pixel Books, we have made it much easier to enjoy your favorite Pixel Books with our Pixel Books Libary Console.

Pixel Books Library Console is a dedicated hardware system that allows your kids to read their favorite books at a touch of a button, anytime they are inspired to read a book. Makes for a perfect gift!

PROCYON NETWORKS

First sole proprietorship setup after my work at GHL. Company set up to install networking for clients including selling of PC and hardware accessories.

Universal Guide and MudTerm was also a couple of projects I developed during this period.

MudTerm Screenshots

Mudterm is a universal game client for MUD (Multi-User Domain) and was developed to bring the world of MUD to narration.

MudTerm was banned by Google for pioneering monetizing apps with ads. MudTerm was created at a time when Google's Advertisement Platform (Adsense) just launched and it was at a time when in-game ads and the concept of it had not existed. It was only many years later after the existence of mobile phone platform did in-game ads became an extremely popular and is now the golden standard of monetizing apps.
